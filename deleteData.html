Data Deletion Request

We understand that you may want to remove your data from SpendiFi. We respect your privacy and are committed to providing you with control over your information. This page allows you to submit a request to delete your SpendiFi account and associated data. Once your request is processed, your account will be deactivated, and your data will be removed from our systems in accordance with our data retention policy.

Important Information: Deleting your account will remove all your data and transaction history within SpendiFi. This action is irreversible. Once your data is deleted, you will not be able to recover it.

How to Submit a Data Deletion Request

Processing Time: Please allow us up to 14 business days to process your data deletion request. Once complete, you will receive a confirmation email notification.

If you have any questions or require further assistance, please contact our Support Team at help.spectraapplications@gmail.com.

We appreciate your understanding.
